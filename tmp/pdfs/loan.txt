1. Stylist Collection Responsibility:
• The stylist is responsible for organising the collection of jewellery pieces via email or Instagram communication.
• Collection costs are to be covered by the stylist.
2. Repair and Replacement Liability:
• The stylist is responsible for the cost of repairing or replacing any lost or damaged pieces.
• The designer must inform the stylist of any previous damages or special care instructions (to be noted in the rental 
form).
• If a piece is beyond repair, the stylist will be charged the full replacement value.
 
3.Immediate Damage Notification:
• The designer must be informed immediately of any damages to or missing items during the rental period.  
4.Jewellery Return:
• All pieces must be returned on the agreed dates unless an extension is agreed upon, in which case the rental form must 
be updated and signed again.  
5.Signed Documentation:
• All rental documentation must be signed and shared with the stylist prior to collection.
 
6.Design Credit:
• All jewellery pieces used must be credited correctly in both print and digital formats.
• Design references must be listed in the rental form for proper attribution.  
7.Image Sharing:
• Any behind-the-scenes or final images featuring Ivy J Studio jewellery must be shared with the designer.
8. Pricing:
• Rental pricing is outlined in a separate Ivy J Studio Rental Pricing List . Please refer to the pricing form for detailed 
rental rates.
